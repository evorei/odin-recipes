Classic Margherita Pizza

Prep: 1 hour Cook: 15 mins Makes: 2 pizzas

Dough Ingredients

  • 3½ cups (500g) bread flour
  • 1¼ cups (300ml) warm water
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 packet (7g) active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Topping Ingredients

  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp grated parmesan
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix yeast, sugar and warm water. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy
  2. Combine flour and salt in large bowl
  3. Add yeast mixture and olive oil to flour, knead for 10 minutes
  4. Cover dough and let rise for 1 hour until doubled
  5. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with pizza stone if available
  6. Divide dough in half and roll out two 12-inch crusts
  7. Spread tomato sauce, add sliced mozzarella
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden
  9. Top with fresh basil, parmesan and a drizzle of olive oil

Chef's Tips

  • For crispier crust, pre-bake for 3 minutes before adding toppings
  • Let dough rest at room temperature for 30 minutes before shaping
  • Use fresh mozzarella packed in water for authentic texture
