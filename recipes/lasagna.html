Classic Lasagna

Prep: 30 mins Cook: 45 mins Serves: 8

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions
  3. Brown ground beef in skillet, drain fat
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, and seasonings in bowl
  5. Layer in 9x13 dish: sauce → noodles → cheese mixture → repeat
  6. Top with mozzarella and parmesan
  7. Bake covered for 25 mins, then uncovered for 20 mins
  8. Let stand 10 mins before serving
